Privacy Policy – Walk College

Walk College uses your personal data solely to enable Facebook login and to match users based on shared hobbies and location.

Data collected:
Name (from your Facebook profile)
Profile picture
Selected hobbies
Device location (used to match users nearby)

We do not share or sell your data to any third parties. All user data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud services).

We do not respond to individual data requests. To stop using the app, simply uninstall it. Inactive user data is automatically cleaned up.

Data Deletion Instructions:
If you want your data deleted immediately, simply uninstall the app. Inactive accounts and their data are removed automatically after 90 days.

Terms of Use – Walk College

1. Purpose: Walk College is a mobile app that connects people with shared hobbies who are located near each other.

2. Facebook Login & Account: The app uses Facebook login to authenticate users. Fake or impersonated accounts may result in a ban.

4. Acceptable Use: You agree not to harass others, upload offensive content, or misuse the app. Abuse may result in suspension.
