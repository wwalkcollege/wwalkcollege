Privacy Policy – Walk College

Walk College uses your personal data solely to enable Facebook login and to match users based on shared hobbies and location.

  • Name (from your Facebook profile)
  • Profile picture
  • Selected hobbies
  • Device location (used to match users nearby)

We do not share or sell your data to any third parties. All user data is stored securely using Firebase (Google Cloud services).

We do not respond to individual data requests. To stop using the app, simply uninstall it. Inactive user data is automatically cleaned up.

Data Deletion Instructions: If you want your data deleted immediately, simply uninstall the app. Inactive accounts and their data are removed automatically after 90 days.

Terms of Use – Walk College

  1. Purpose: Walk College is a mobile app that connects people with shared hobbies who are located near each other. Its goal is to help users find walking buddies and social activity partners based on mutual interests and location.
  2. Facebook Login & Account: The app uses Facebook login to authenticate users. You are responsible for providing accurate information via your Facebook account. Fake or impersonated accounts may result in a ban.
  3. Location & Hobby Matching: The app uses your device’s location and your selected hobbies to match you with nearby users who share similar interests. You control which hobbies to share. Location is used only for proximity-based suggestions.
  4. Acceptable Use: You agree to use the app respectfully and not to:
    • Harass or insult other users
    • Upload offensive content
    • Misuse the app in any way
    Any abusive behavior may result in immediate suspension or removal of your account.
